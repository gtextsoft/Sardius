Your Ownership Is Protected

Sardius Group Estate prioritizes title clarity and transparent sales. We provide full buyer documentation and encourage independent verification.

What You Receive

Documents You Receive

Every Sardius landowner receives comprehensive documentation that protects their investment and proves their ownership. We encourage you to verify all documents independently.

  • ✓ Survey Plan – Detailed map showing your plot boundaries and location
  • ✓ Deed of Assignment – Legal transfer of ownership rights to you
  • ✓ Contract of Sale – Terms and conditions of your purchase
  • ✓ Receipt – Proof of payment for your records
  • ✓ Estate Approvals – Relevant government approvals per estate

Important Notice

While we provide verified documentation for all our properties, we strongly encourage all buyers to engage their own legal counsel to conduct independent verification. This protects your investment and gives you complete peace of mind.

Our team is happy to cooperate with your lawyer and provide any additional information needed for your due diligence process.
