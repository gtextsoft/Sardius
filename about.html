Who We Are

About Sardius Group Estate

Sardius Group Estate is a high-value estate portfolio developed by Gtext Land / Gtext Holdings to provide green, smart, and luxury real estate options in carefully selected Nigerian cities.

Our estates are designed for people who want more than land — they want secure ownership, lifestyle quality, and strong investment outcomes. Before any Sardius plot is offered for sale, it goes through strict verification for title clarity and development viability.

Every Sardius location is chosen for title safety, accessibility, lifestyle quality, and future value growth. We partner with you from inspection to allocation, ensuring a seamless property acquisition experience.

Our Mission

To deliver secure, smart, and eco-friendly estates that enable families live better and investors earn more. We are committed to providing premium real estate solutions that combine affordability with luxury.

Our Vision

To become a top real estate reference brand for premium communities in Nigeria's best corridors. We aim to set the standard for estate development that balances luxury, sustainability, and accessibility.
